Select a mode, then press START. The rover drives the chosen mathematical path and paints a faint "cleaned" trail. Every frame it checks the distance from its sensor radius to each buried metal object — on intersection it performs an immediate Emergency Halt (mirroring the 555-timer interrupt that sends the 'S' stop byte over the HC-05 link to the Arduino).
